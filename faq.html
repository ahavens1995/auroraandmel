F.A.Q.

  • Do I have to dress up?
    • Yes, all of our guests are required to don their best renaissance fair,medieval, or fantasy attire to our event. Should you need inspiration, please visit the Dress Code Page for examples and stores.
  • Where will the Reception be held?
    • Reception will be held at the same place as the ceremony.
  • My outfit has elements of blue, white, or green in it. Can I still wear it?
    • Please reach out to the brides with pictures for final approval.
  • Do you have a registry?
    • We do not. Our guests dressing up and attending our wedding is great gift.
  • Are children allowed?
    • No, children are not allowed at the wedding.
